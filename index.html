Welcome to the project page of next

Next is a game that present a variaty of numerical series that have to be continued. By solving each puzzle you can improve your logical thought. Therby barely no mathematical foreknowledge is necessary.

About

Next was developed as an exercise for my CS studies.
Next does save your progress on your smart phone. No data is sent to any server. If you are looking for a formal privacy policy click here: Privacy
